About Us

Our story, mission, and commitment to providing quality tech and excellent service.

Our Story

Founded in 2020, Tsailun Technologies began with a simple mission: to provide high-quality, reliable, and affordable refurbished technology to the Nairobi community. We saw a need for a sustainable alternative to brand-new devices and built our business on the principles of quality, transparency, and customer satisfaction.

Our Mission

We are dedicated to offering the best refurbished laptops, desktops, and accessories. Our team of certified technicians meticulously inspects and restores every device, ensuring it meets the highest standards of performance and reliability. By giving tech a second life, we also contribute to a greener planet by reducing electronic waste.

Our Values

  • Quality: We never compromise on the quality of our products.
  • Integrity: We believe in honest pricing and transparent business practices.
  • Sustainability: We are committed to reducing our environmental impact.
  • Customer Focus: Your satisfaction is our top priority.
