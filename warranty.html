Warranty & Returns

Information on our product warranties, return policy, and how to start a claim.

1. Our Warranty Policy

All our refurbished laptops and desktops come with a **standard 1-year hardware warranty**, effective from the date of purchase. This warranty covers defects in materials and workmanship under normal use. It does not cover accidental damage, software issues, or unauthorized repairs.

2. What is Covered?

  • Hardware components such as the motherboard, CPU, RAM, and storage drive.
  • Display issues not caused by physical damage.
  • Battery performance (subject to normal degradation).

3. What is NOT Covered?

  • Accidental damage (drops, liquid spills, etc.).
  • Damage caused by misuse or improper care.
  • Software problems, viruses, or data loss.
  • Consumable parts like batteries and chargers beyond initial testing.

4. Our Returns Policy

We offer a **7-day return policy** on all products. If you are not completely satisfied with your purchase, you may return the item for a full refund or exchange. The product must be returned in the same condition it was received, with all original packaging and accessories.

5. How to Initiate a Return or Warranty Claim

To start a return or warranty claim, please contact our support team with your order number and a detailed description of the issue. Our team will guide you through the next steps.
